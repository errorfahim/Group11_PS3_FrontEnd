About the Integrated Vaccination System

The Integrated Vaccination System of Bangladesh aims to provide comprehensive vaccination coverage for all citizens. Our mission is to ensure that everyone has access to life-saving vaccines and to improve public health outcomes across the country.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a vaccination appointment?

You can book a vaccination appointment online through our official website or by calling our help center.

What vaccines are available?

We offer a range of vaccines including COVID-19, flu, hepatitis B, measles, mumps, rubella, polio, HPV, tetanus, and chickenpox vaccines.

Are the vaccines free?

Yes, all vaccines provided by the Integrated Vaccination System are free of charge for all citizens.
